Our Story

Pioneering Progress: A Journey of Innovation and Sustainability

National Plastics, one of the high quality plastic manufacturing industry in Kerala was established in 2005. Ever since its arrival it has brought accolades globally and has satisfied the market and the consumer piously by adapting to the ever-changing consumer needs and demands. With its products spanning almost all of the household plastic inventory. The company has ensured quality in each and very produce. The goal is to revolutionise the market with high quality in demand products at the most fairest of prices. The company has also been keen on global impact of plastic and has been using only the most eco-friendly technologies to ensure sustainability.
